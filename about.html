ABOUT US

About AMR

For the past ten years, we have been committed to providing quality construction, restoration, and engineering services to our clients through professionalism, quality workmanship, and reliability. Our experience, integrity, and commitment to quality provide our clients with the most receptive, cooperative and cost-effective services. We are proud to work with Choice Properties, TFI International, Aspen Properties, Stoney Tribal Administration, Le Germain Hotel, Metis Urban Housing Corporation, London Drugs, and many more clients. We are proud to have built on long-term relationships with our clients and continue to achieve the trust of our future clients.
We believe that client satisfaction is the foundation of our success. The client’s success is ultimately our success. It plays a key role in our business because it affects our projects along with our teams. We strive to build trust and loyalty by keeping our clients informed at every step of the project, being honest and ensuring that the job is done in a safe environment.
