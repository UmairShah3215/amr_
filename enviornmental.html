Providing clients with honest advice for their environmental engineering projects

AMR provides environmental and engineering solutions for a wide range of projects including remediation and local waste management services. We use innovative techniques to address complex contamination problems while keeping our client requirements and regulatory compliance a priority in every project..
AMR strives to focus on the need of our clients and aims to reduce environmental impacts in order to create a positive impact in the environment and our communities.

Treatment methods are divided into soil surface and groundwater remediation:

  1. |Environmental assessment and planning
  2. |Environmental protection planning

The majority of techniques are categorized as physical treatments which involve removal of hazard through physical means such as capping, cementitious waste forms, and etc. Though, Biodegradation falls under the biological treatment category. Furthermore, chemical treatments involve the application to promote extraction of the hazardous substance. Hydrocarbon contaminants are removed from soil by bioremediation and volatilization.
